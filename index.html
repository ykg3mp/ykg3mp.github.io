About Us

We at cat-cafe, have noticed that there are many websites explaining how to adopt and take care of cats for humans. But, there are very few websites explaining how to adopt and take care of humans for cats. In 2020, our team of talented catornalists, catographers, catigners, and catogrammers came together to create a website to help cats through the process of adopting humans. We hope you are enjoy a sip of milk or a lap of water while browsing through our guides or fun facts below. Miao-miao!
